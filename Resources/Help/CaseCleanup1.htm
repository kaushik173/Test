Administration - Case Cleanup

The Case Cleanup page includes the following quality assurance categories.

  • Cases without a Child
  • Open Cases without a Department
  • Cases without a Client
  • Cases without a Petition
  • Cases without a Case Name
  • Open Petitions without a Client
  • Open Cases without an Active Attorney
  • Cases with a Duplicate Case #
  • Open Cases without a Parent
  • Open Cases - No Future Date (Last Date: 14 to 365 Days Ago)
  • Open Cases - No Future Date (Last Date: Over a Year Ago)


Rule: A case can only exist in one category at a time.  The categories are listed in the order of priority.  Therefore, as an example, if a case is without a child and without a department then it will only display in the Cases without a Child category.

Sorting: The cases within each category are sorted by the Inserted On column, with exception to the No Future Date categories, which are sorted by the Attorney and Jcats # columns.
