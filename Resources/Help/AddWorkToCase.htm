Data Entry - Record Time Add/List

Recording time constists of two pages, a List and an Add/Edit page.

List Page: The list page displays all time recorded on the case and is sorted by date in descending order.  You can filter the display by using the Worker and/or Client dropdowns.  The description and "Add Time" links are used for updating and adding time respectively. 

To print the time recorded, simply check the desired records and click the "Print" link at the top of column one.  The checkbox in the column header is the toggle switch used to check all and uncheck all of the checkboxes.

Add/Edit Page: The Worker dropdown includes all staff members that play a role on the case.  the Fill In Staff dropdown includes all staff memebers that do not play a role on the case.  The "Worked For" section includes all clients.  By default all the clients are checked, unless they are no longer on an active petition, in which case it displays this fact in column two. i.e. All Petitions Closed - C171717.
