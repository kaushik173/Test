Data Entry - Add Legal Parties to Case

The page consists of three sections: Role Select, Role Freeform, and Legal Parties In Case List.

Role Selection: This section allows you to add the following legal party role types to the case: Staff Attorneys, Other Agency Attorneys, Judges, County Counsel, Investigators/Social Workers, and Paralegals.  The names are contained in their respective role type dropdown.  If the name you are looking for is not in the dropdown then contact your Jcats administrator for assistance.

Role Freefrom Entry: This section allows you to add other legal party role types to the case, by selecting the role type and entering their last name and first name.  This section also allows you to enter the person's address and contact information, their association to the children/parents (if applicable), and which children/parents are living with them (if applicable).

Legal Parties in Case List: This section is a list of all the legal parties that have been added to the case.

Note: Use the Transfer button to transfer the case to another Staff Attorney.  This will route you to the Transfer Case page where you can select the newly assigned staff attorney.
