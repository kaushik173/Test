Inquiry - MyJcats - Supv Atty

This is the home page for supervising attorneys, which means they are automatically routed to this page upon login.  The page is divided into the following four sections.

Search (left section): The date range is used by the My Agenda and To Do sections, because they return the hearings and notifications within the date range.  Use the Case Search fields to quickly route to the Main Page for any of the cases in the database.  If your search matches more than one case then you will be routed to the Search for Case page where all matching cases will be displayed.  You can then select the desired case.

My Agenda (top right section): displays the list of attorneys that have hearings scheduled during the date range, sorted by attorney name.  The Attorney link routes to the My Calendar page, which is the page each individual attorney uses to view and print their own calendar.

To Do (bottom middle section): displays your notifications, as well as quick links to your My Caseload page.

My Reports (bottom right section): This displays your favorite reports, so you can quickly access them.  You mantain this list by clicking the My Reports header, which routes you to the My Reports page.  The My Reports page displays all reports that you have access to and allows you to choose which ones you want on your MyJcats page.
