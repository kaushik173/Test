Inquiry - MyJcats - Inv/Paralegal

This is the home page for investigators/paralegals/social workers, which means they are automatically routed to this page upon login.  The page is divided into the following four sections.

Search (left section): The date range is used by the To Do section, because it returns all notifications due prior to or within the date range.  Use the Case Search fields to quickly route to the Main Page for any of the cases in the database.  If your search matches more than one case then you will be routed to the Search for Case page where all matching cases will be displayed.  You can then select the desired case.

My Agenda (top right section): displays all action requests due, sorted by due date.  The Type link routes to the AR Edit page, which is part of a submenu that includes the ability to enter/update addresses, profile questionnaires, and time worked.

To Do (bottom middle section): displays your notifications, as well as quick a link to your My AR Queue page.

My Reports (bottom right section): This displays your favorite reports, so you can quickly access them.  You mantain this list by clicking the My Reports header, which routes you to the My Reports page.  The My Reports page displays all reports that you have access to and allows you to choose which ones you want on your MyJcats page.
