Tasks - AR Summary

The page summarizes the outstanding action requests (AR) for all staff members.  Staff are displayed alphabetically, grouped by position.  The outstanding AR's are broken down into the following six categories:

  • Past Due
  • Due within 7 Days
  • Due within 30 Days
  • Due within 90 Days
  • Due within 180 Days
  • Due after 180 Days


Inactive staff members will display when the "Include Inactive Staff" checkbox is checked, which is the default.  Inactive staff member names are preceeded with an asterisk (*).

To access an individual staff member's action request queue, use the Attorney hyper-link, which routes to the My AR Queue page.
