Inquiry - MyJcats - Atty

This is the home page for attorneys, which means they are automatically routed to this page upon login.  The page is divided into the following four sections.

Search (left section): The date range is used by the My Agenda and To Do sections, because they return the hearings and notifications within the date range.  Use the My Open Case Search fields to quickly route to the Main Page for any of your open cases.  If your open case search matches more than one case then you will be routed to your My Caseload page where all matching cases will be displayed.  You can then select the desired case.

My Agenda (top right section): displays all hearings during the selected date range, sorted chronologically.  The Type link routes to the Main Page of the case, and the QHE (Quick Hearing Entry) link routes to the QHE sub menu pages.

To Do (bottom middle section): displays your notifications, as well as quick links to your My Caseload and My Action Requests pages.

My Reports (bottom right section): This displays your favorite reports, so you can quickly access them.  You mantain this list by clicking the My Reports header, which routes you to the My Reports page.  The My Reports page displays all reports that you have access to and allows you to choose which ones you want on your MyJcats page.
